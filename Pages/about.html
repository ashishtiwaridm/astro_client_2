About Future Point

Future Point Pvt Ltd was started in 1985 by Mr. Arun Bansal. He is M.Sc. in Physics from Delhi University and has also done M.Phil & Research in Computer Science from Jawahar Lal Nehru University, Delhi. Mr. Arun Bansal developed the first comprehensive software on Astrology in India way back in 1978. New dimensions were added to Astrology by Future Point by introducing modern techniques of mathematics and computers. Future Point has actually programmed computers not only for accurate horoscope casting and correct predictions but has also tested their validity by using advanced mathematics.

The basic aim of the company

  • To promote and standardize astrology and other allied sciences internationally.
  • To serve the mankind by utilizing the science and making everyone aware of all the new developments taking place in this field.
  • To promote research work in the field of astrology and other predictive sciences.

To achieve its aim Future Point has released a series of astrological software starting from DOS technology to Windows, XP, and Vista. It has also developed complete astrology software for Palm organizer based on Palm OS and Microsoft mobile windows.

Hence it has facilitated astrologers with a complete astrological tool in their pocket useful for casting horoscope anywhere, anytime. It is also publishing Future Samachar, a monthly astrological magazine in Hindi since 1995 which is very popular all over India and abroad and contains very good articles on astrology. To spread the knowledge of these sciences to the masses, Future Point invited societies from all over India to form All India Federation of Astrologers Societies in 2001. All India Federation of Astrologers Societies has established more than 100 chapters in India and abroad.

Courses on Astrology, Vastu, Palmistry, Numerology, and Tarot were introduced, and more than fifteen thousand students have learnt these esoteric subjects from All India Federation of Astrologers Societies. To promote the researches in this field, it also started the publication of the bilingual quarterly magazine Research Journal of Astrology. Future Point entered into the field of Remedial Measures to provide genuine remedial and spiritual products under one roof. To facilitate students, Future Point has published books on more than 30 titles of Astrology, Vastu, Palmistry, Lal Kitab, Remedies, and Numerology. Its Encyclopedia of Astrological Remedies has been liked and greatly appreciated by all.

Provides personal consultancy on Astrology, Palmistry, and Vastu in person and on the net. To promote Astrology, Future point has successfully organized International Astrological conferences in Mauritius, Netherlands, Bangkok, and Singapore. At the national level also it is organizing many conferences all over India. Nakshatra exhibition is also organized by Future Point with India Trade Promotion Organisation (ITPO) every year in Pragati Maidan from the last five years.
